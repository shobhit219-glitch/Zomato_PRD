# Zomato Multi-Restaurant Ordering PRD

## Project Overview

This portfolio contains a comprehensive **Product Requirements Document (PRD)** for implementing a **Multi-Restaurant Ordering** feature at Zomato.

## 📋 Document Contents

### Multi-Restaurant-Ordering-PRD.md

A detailed 18-section PRD covering:

1. **Executive Summary** - Business goals and expected impact
2. **Problem Statement** - Current pain points and market opportunities
3. **Goals & Objectives** - OKRs and success metrics
4. **User Stories** - 5 main user stories with acceptance criteria
5. **Feature Specification** - Core and secondary features
   - Multi-restaurant mode toggle
   - Unified shopping cart
   - Intelligent checkout flow
   - Order tracking & delivery
   - Post-delivery experience
6. **UX & Design Considerations** - Design principles and onboarding
7. **Technical Architecture** - Backend changes, APIs, and frontend components
8. **Success Metrics & KPIs** - Business, user, operational, and quality metrics
9. **Risks & Mitigation** - 7 key risks with mitigation strategies
10. **Go-to-Market Strategy** - 6-phase rollout plan (32 weeks)
11. **Competitive Analysis** - Comparison with Swiggy and DoorDash
12. **Dependencies & Cross-Team Requirements** - Internal and external dependencies
13. **Resource & Budget Estimation** - Team structure and $800K-$1.5M budget
14. **Success Criteria** - MVP and launch success criteria
15. **Out of Scope** - Features deferred to later phases
16. **Use Cases** - Family dinner and office lunch scenarios
17. **Appendices** - Architecture diagram, compliance, glossary
18. **Sign-offs & Approval** - Documentation framework

## 🎯 Key Highlights

- **Expected AOV Increase:** 15-25%
- **Adoption Target:** 35% of DAU in 3 months
- **MVP Timeline:** 16 weeks development
- **6-Phase Rollout:** Research → Development → Testing → Closed Beta → Wider Beta → GA
- **Budget:** $800K - $1.5M

## 👥 Target Users

1. **Group Organizers** - Office groups and families
2. **Indecisive Users** - Want variety in one order
3. **Deal Seekers** - Optimize spending across restaurants

## 🏗️ Architecture Overview

- Single delivery partner for coordinated pickups
- Order aggregation service
- Delivery routing optimization
- Unified payment and checkout
- Multi-restaurant tracking interface

## 🚀 Rollout Phases

1. **Phase 1** (Weeks 1-4): Research & Validation
2. **Phase 2** (Weeks 5-16): MVP Development (2-restaurant max)
3. **Phase 3** (Weeks 17-20): Internal Testing
4. **Phase 4** (Weeks 21-24): Closed Beta (10K users, 3-4 cities)
5. **Phase 5** (Weeks 25-32): Wider Beta (50K users, expanded features)
6. **Phase 6** (Week 33+): General Availability

## ⚠️ Key Risks Addressed

1. Delivery operational complexity
2. Restaurant partner resistance
3. Order cancellation coordination
4. User confusion and support burden
5. Technology and system limitations
6. Reduced per-restaurant revenue
7. Delivery surge pricing impact
